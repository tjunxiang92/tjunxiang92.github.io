    Coterie strives to enhance your experience as a student by bringing the best opportunities to you. Knowing your passions and interest, we bring an idea to the table where you and a group of talented peers connect and build on it with no strings attached.

Why

Expand Your Networks

Meet new friends across institutions, bringing different experiences, opinions, and ideas which are bound to broaden your perspectives through such interactions.

Personalized Specially For You

Every team that we form is carefully handcrafted and vetted by us individually. We make sure that we connect people that will be most beneficial to you for both your learning and experience.
-- We take feedback very seriously and will be constantly finding new ways to improve our vetting processes to your satisfaction. --

Test Your Limits

Expose yourself to unfamiliar territory as you hustle through obstacles and problems big and small. Afterall, you will never know what you can achieve if you don't try.

Make an Impact

"Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you'll land among the stars."

Never be afraid to dream big. At Coterie, we believe that big changes in the world always start with a small step. Have faith and enjoy the journey.
